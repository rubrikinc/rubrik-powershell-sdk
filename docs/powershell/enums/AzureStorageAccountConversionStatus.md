### AzureStorageAccountConversionStatus
Conversion status for a storage account redundancy migration.

- AZURE_STORAGE_ACCOUNT_CONVERSION_STATUS_NONE - No conversion in progress or previously completed.
- AZURE_STORAGE_ACCOUNT_CONVERSION_STATUS_SUBMITTED - Conversion has been submitted and is pending.
- AZURE_STORAGE_ACCOUNT_CONVERSION_STATUS_IN_PROGRESS - Conversion is actively in progress.
- AZURE_STORAGE_ACCOUNT_CONVERSION_STATUS_SUCCEEDED - Conversion completed successfully.
- AZURE_STORAGE_ACCOUNT_CONVERSION_STATUS_FAILED - Conversion failed.
