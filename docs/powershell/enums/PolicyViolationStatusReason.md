### PolicyViolationStatusReason
Represents the reason for a policy violation's last status change.

- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_REASON_UNSPECIFIED - Unspecified reason.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_REASON_OPEN - The violation was opened.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_REASON_ASSET_DELETED - The resource was deleted.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_REASON_DISMISSED - The violation was dismissed.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_REASON_POLICY_MODIFIED - The policy was modified.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_REASON_POLICY_DELETED - The policy was deleted.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_REASON_POLICY_DISABLED - The policy was disabled.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_REASON_POLICY_OTHER - The policy was modified internally.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_REASON_ASSET_UNCLASSIFIABLE - The asset is unclassifiable.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_REASON_REMEDIATED - The violation was manually remediated (manual revert).
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_REASON_AUTO_REMEDIATED - The violation was automatically remediated (auto-revert).
