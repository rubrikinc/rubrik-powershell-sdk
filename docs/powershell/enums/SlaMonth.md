### SlaMonth
Supported in v5.0+
The month of the year when snapshot will be taken.

- SLA_MONTH_APRIL - April.
- SLA_MONTH_AUGUST - August.
- SLA_MONTH_DECEMBER - December.
- SLA_MONTH_FEBRUARY - February.
- SLA_MONTH_JANUARY - January.
- SLA_MONTH_JULY - July.
- SLA_MONTH_JUNE - June.
- SLA_MONTH_MARCH - March.
- SLA_MONTH_MAY - May.
- SLA_MONTH_NOVEMBER - November.
- SLA_MONTH_OCTOBER - October.
- SLA_MONTH_SEPTEMBER - September.
