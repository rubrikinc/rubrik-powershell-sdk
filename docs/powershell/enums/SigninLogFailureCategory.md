### SigninLogFailureCategory
Category of a sign-in error.

- SIGNIN_LOG_FAILURE_CATEGORY_INVALID_CREDENTIALS - Wrong or unknown credentials.
- SIGNIN_LOG_FAILURE_CATEGORY_ACCOUNT_LOCKED - Account locked out.
- SIGNIN_LOG_FAILURE_CATEGORY_ACCOUNT_DISABLED - Account inactive or revoked.
- SIGNIN_LOG_FAILURE_CATEGORY_PASSWORD_EXPIRED - Password expired or must be changed.
- SIGNIN_LOG_FAILURE_CATEGORY_MFA_FAILURE - Multi-factor authentication failed.
- SIGNIN_LOG_FAILURE_CATEGORY_POLICY_BLOCKED - Blocked by access or compliance policy.
- SIGNIN_LOG_FAILURE_CATEGORY_OTHER - Failure that does not map to another category.
