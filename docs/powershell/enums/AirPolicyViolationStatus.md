### AirPolicyViolationStatus
Policy violation status.

- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_UNSPECIFIED - Policy violation status is unspecified.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_OPEN - Policy violation is open.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_REMEDIATED - Policy violation is remediated.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_DISMISSED - Policy violation is dismissed.
- POLICY_VIOLATION_STATUS_IN_PROGRESS - Policy violation is in progress.
