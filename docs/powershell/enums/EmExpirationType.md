### EmExpirationType
Expiration mode for access package assignments.

- EM_EXPIRATION_TYPE_UNSPECIFIED - Expiration type is unspecified.
- EM_EXPIRATION_TYPE_AFTER_DURATION - Expires after a fixed duration.
- EM_EXPIRATION_TYPE_AFTER_DATE_TIME - Expires at a specific date and time.
- EM_EXPIRATION_TYPE_NO_EXPIRATION - Does not expire.
