### PolicyType
Policy type captures the type of policy that is being evaluated.

- POLICY_TYPE_UNSPECIFIED - Unspecified policy type.
- POLICY_TYPE_DATAGOV - Data discovery policy type.
- POLICY_TYPE_IDENTITY - Identity policy type.
- POLICY_TYPE_IDP - Identity Provider (IdP) policy type.
For AD, Okta, EntraID, AWS Identity center etc.
- POLICY_TYPE_IDENTITY_EVENT - Identity event policy type.
For identity-related events, such as changes to group memberships and GPOs
- POLICY_TYPE_CROWDSTRIKE - Crowdstrike policy type.
- POLICY_TYPE_MICROSOFT_DEFENDER - Microsoft Defender for Identity policy type.
