### IntuneAssignmentFilterManagementType
Specifies the management type of an Intune assignment filter.

- INTUNE_ASSIGNMENT_FILTER_MANAGEMENT_TYPE_UNKNOWN - The management type is unknown.
- INTUNE_ASSIGNMENT_FILTER_MANAGEMENT_TYPE_DEVICES - The management type is managed devices.
- INTUNE_ASSIGNMENT_FILTER_MANAGEMENT_TYPE_APPS - The management type is managed apps.
