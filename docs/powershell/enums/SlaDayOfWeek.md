### SlaDayOfWeek
Supported in v5.0+
The day of the week when snapshot will be taken.

- SLA_DAY_OF_WEEK_FRIDAY - Friday.
- SLA_DAY_OF_WEEK_MONDAY - Monday.
- SLA_DAY_OF_WEEK_SATURDAY - Saturday.
- SLA_DAY_OF_WEEK_SUNDAY - Sunday.
- SLA_DAY_OF_WEEK_THURSDAY - Thursday.
- SLA_DAY_OF_WEEK_TUESDAY - Tuesday.
- SLA_DAY_OF_WEEK_WEDNESDAY - Wednesday.
