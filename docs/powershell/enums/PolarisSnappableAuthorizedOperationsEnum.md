### PolarisSnappableAuthorizedOperationsEnum
Authorized operations on protectable objects.

- MANAGE_PROTECTION - Manage protection operation.
- VIEW_INVENTORY - View inventory operation.
- RESTORE_TO_ORIGIN - Restore to origin operation.
- EXPORT_SNAPSHOTS - Export snapshots operation.
- TAKE_ON_DEMAND_SNAPSHOT - Take on demand snapshot operation.
- DELETE_SNAPSHOT - Delete snapshot operation.
- DOWNLOAD - Download operation.
- MOUNT - Mount operation.
