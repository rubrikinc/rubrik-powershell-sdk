### MonthlyDaySpecInput
Specification for day selection for monthly snapshot schedule. You can choose only one type of monthly specification: 
1. Choose the date offset for the month. For example, 1 specifies first day of the month and -1 would mean last day of the month. 
2. Choose the day of week pattern. For example, First Monday, Last Friday. 


- spec: MonthlyDaySpecSpecInput
  - The monthly day specification.
