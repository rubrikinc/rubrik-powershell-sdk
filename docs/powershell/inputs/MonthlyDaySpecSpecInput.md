### MonthlyDaySpecSpecInput
The day specification for the monthly snapshot schedule can be either a specific date or a day-of-week pattern.

- specificDate: MonthlyDaySpecSpecificDateInput
  - Input for selecting a specific date in a month to schedule a snapshot.
- dayOfWeekInMonth: MonthlyDaySpecDayOfWeekPatternInput
  - Input for selecting a specific day of the week in a month to schedule a snapshot.
