### IntuneRoleAssignment
Intune role assignment.

- displayName: System.String
  - Display name of the role assignment.
- description: System.String
  - Description of the role assignment.
- roleDefinitionId: System.String
  - ID of the role definition.
- scopeType: System.String
  - Scope type of the role assignment.
- members: list of IntuneRoleAssignmentObjectIdentifiers
  - Members of the role assignment.
- resourceScopes: list of IntuneRoleAssignmentObjectIdentifiers
  - Resource scopes of the role assignment.
- roleScopeTagNames: list of System.Strings
  - Role scope tag names associated with the role assignment.
- roleScopeTagIds: list of System.Strings
  - Role scope tag IDs associated with the role assignment.
