### IntuneAppProtectionPolicy
Intune app protection policy.

- displayName: System.String
  - Display name of the app protection policy.
- platform: IntuneDevicePlatformType
  - Platform type of the app protection policy.
- managementType: IntuneAppProtectionManagementType
  - Management type of the app protection policy.
- isDeployed: System.Boolean
  - Specifies whether the policy is deployed.
- lastModifiedDateTime: DateTime
  - Date and time when the policy was last modified.
- roleScopeTagNames: list of System.Strings
  - Role scope tag names associated with the policy.
- roleScopeTagIds: list of System.Strings
  - Role scope tag IDs associated with the policy.
