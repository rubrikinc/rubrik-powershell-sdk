### IdentityDetails
The details of an identity.

- principalType: PrincipalRiskySummaryPrincipalType
  - The principal type of the identity.
- idpType: IdpType
  - The IdP type of the identity.
- source: EntitySource
  - The source of the identity.
- privilegeType: PrivilegeType
  - The privilege type of the identity.
