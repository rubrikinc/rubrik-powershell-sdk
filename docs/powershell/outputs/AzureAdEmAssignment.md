### AzureAdEmAssignment
Entitlement Management assignment.

- displayName: System.String
  - Display name of the assigned principal.
- principalType: EmSubjectType
  - Type of the subject (accessPackageSubjectType from Microsoft Graph API).
- userPrincipalName: System.String
  - UPN of the assignee.
- startDate: DateTime
  - Date when the assignment becomes active.
- endDate: DateTime
  - Date when the assignment expires.
