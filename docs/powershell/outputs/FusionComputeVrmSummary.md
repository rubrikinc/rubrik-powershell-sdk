### FusionComputeVrmSummary
Summary information for a FusionCompute Virtual Resource Management (VRM) instance.

- endpointUri: System.String
  - Required. The address of the FusionCompute VRM instance.
- slaAssignable: SlaAssignable
  - Details of the SLA Domain assigned to the FusionCompute Virtual Resource Management (VRM).
- username: System.String
  - Required. The username of the FusionCompute VRM instance.
