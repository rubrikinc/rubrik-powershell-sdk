### IntuneCompliancePolicy
Intune device compliance policy.

- displayName: System.String
  - Display name of the Intune compliance policy.
- platform: IntuneCompliancePolicyPlatform
  - Platform type of the Intune compliance policy.
- policyType: IntuneCompliancePolicyType
  - Policy type of the Intune compliance policy.
- scriptName: System.String
  - Name of the script associated with the Intune compliance policy.
- roleScopeTags: list of System.Strings
  - Role scope tags associated with the Intune compliance policy.
- version: System.String
  - Version of the Intune compliance policy.
- lastModifiedTime: DateTime
  - Date and time when the Intune compliance policy was last modified.
