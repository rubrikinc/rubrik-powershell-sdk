### NasShareDetail
Supported in v8.1+

- id: System.String
  - Required. Supported in v8.1+
  The unique ID of the NAS Share.
- exportPoint: System.String
  - Required. Supported in v8.1+
  The NFS export point or SMB share name for the NAS share.
- shareType: NasShareDetailShareType
  - Type of the NAS share.
