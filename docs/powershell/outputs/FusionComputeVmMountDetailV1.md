### FusionComputeVmMountDetailV1
Detailed information for a FusionCompute Live Mount.

- powerStatus: System.String
  - The power status of the mounted virtual machine (such as ON or OFF).
- nasIp: System.String
  - The IP address of the NFS share.
- fusionComputeVmMountSummaryV1: FusionComputeVmMountSummaryV1
  - Summary fields for the Live Mount.
