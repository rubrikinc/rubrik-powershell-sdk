### WeeklySnapshotSchedule
Weekly snapshot schedule.

- basicSchedule: BasicSnapshotSchedule
  - Basic weekly snapshot schedule.
- dayOfWeek: DayOfWeek
  - Day of the week.
- daysOfWeek: list of WeeklyDaySpecs
  - List of days of the week on which we want snapshots to be taken for the weekly frequency (1-7 days).
