### pendingAction
A pending action.

- description: System.String
  - The description of the pending action.
- actionType: PendingActionType
  - The type of the pending action.
- pendingActionId: System.String
  - The ID of the pending action.
- clusterUuid: System.String
  - The UUID of the cluster.
- status: PendingActionStatus
  - The status of the pending action.
- createdAt: DateTime
  - The time when the pending action was created.
- updatedAt: DateTime
  - The time when the pending action was last updated.
- info: System.String
  - Additional information about the pending action.
- actionTypeStr: System.String
  - The string representation of the action type.
