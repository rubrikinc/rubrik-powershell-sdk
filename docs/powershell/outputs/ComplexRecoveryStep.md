### ComplexRecoveryStep
A single step in a complex recovery.

- simpleSteps: RecoverySteps
  - The recovery steps for this step.
- status: RecoveryStatus
  - Status of this recovery step.
- recoveryOutcome: RecoveryOutcome
  - Outcome of this recovery step.
- recoveryName: System.String
  - Name of the recovery.
- recoveryId: System.String
  - ID of the recovery.
- workloadType: ManagedObjectType
  - Workload type of the recovery.
